Tree of Life

The Tree of Life signifies many things, one of them being a fresh start in life, which we hope it brings you when needed.
We include the tree of life in various pieces of our jewelry.
Some of the trees are hand-made by wire wrapping and glass beads, some of them appear in the form of a charm.
