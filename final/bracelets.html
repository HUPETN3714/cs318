Bracelets

There are a variety of bracelets that we make, including the popular flower braclet (pictured left), beaded bracelets, and wire-wrap charm bracelets.
Each bracelet is hand-made and unique.
We use glass beads, high-quality metal tubing and charms, and memory wire throughout our various bracelets.
Charm bracelets are customizeable with our collection of charms ranging from sports to pets.
Bracelets can be stacked or worn alone!
