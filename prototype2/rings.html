Wire Rings

Our Wire Rings are the very first thing we made here at Hartstrings Creations.
Each ring is hand wrapped using various gauges of wire and glass beads so no two rings are exactly alike.
We offer various sizes at our shows, and rings can be ordered to specific size.
Lightweight design allows the rings to be worn everyday.
