Interchangeable Necklaces

Our unique interchangeable necklaces provide a fun way to change up your necklaces.
You can buy the simple chain necklace and we offer various charm clusters and stones to change the style.
Switching between charm clusters is made easy by using a T-bar and O-ring. Each charm cluster is unique and hand made with glass beads.
Made to fit any style!
